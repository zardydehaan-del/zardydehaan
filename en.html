"Movement is my language"

I grew up in Leeuwarden, and between the ages of 8 and 14, you'd rarely find me without a skateboard underfoot. Those years taught me something I still use every single day: how to read an environment — and how to push yourself one step further than you thought possible.

In 2016, I discovered freerunning. It felt like coming home. What started as movement for the sake of movement slowly grew into something larger: a way to tell stories with my body.

Today I work as a stuntman and model, at the intersection of action and visual language. I don't pose and wait for the shutter — I execute a jump, a fall, a movement so it lands. Technically and visually. The Silver Medal at the Zilveren Camera Awards, alongside photographer Marc, proved what becomes possible when creator and performer are truly aligned. That's exactly the kind of collaboration I'm after.
